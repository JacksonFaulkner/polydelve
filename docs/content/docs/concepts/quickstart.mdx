## Place your first bet

### Step 1 — Log in

Open the app and click **Sign In** in the top-right navbar. Auth0 handles authentication. If this is your first visit, you will land on the **UsernameModal** after login — pick a username (3–20 chars, letters/numbers/underscores) before continuing.

> Unauthenticated users who click **Bet** on any market card are redirected to the Auth0 login page automatically. Complete login and you will be returned to the contract.

### Step 2 — Find a package

The home feed shows the **Market Spotlight** (top featured contract) and a grid of active markets below it, each ranked by `relevancy_score`. Use the sector tabs (**npm**, **PyPI**) to filter by ecosystem, or go to **Predict** to search by name.

![Home feed showing Market Spotlight and market grid](https://assets.polydelve.com/screenshots/qs-1-home.png)

### Step 3 — Gauge risk with the EPSS chart

Each market card shows an EPSS trend chart. The green line plots daily EPSS scores; orange dots mark past CVE publication dates on the same axis.

- **Higher EPSS** → higher probability of exploitation → higher contract cost and higher potential payout.
- A rising trend line signals the community considers this package increasingly exploitable.

Use the chart to decide whether the price is worth the risk before committing Schmeckles.

### Step 4 — Configure and buy

**Option A — Market card.** Click **Bet** directly on any market card. Price and payout multiplier are pre-set based on the current EPSS score.

**Option B — Predict page.** Navigate to **Predict**, search for a package, and dial in your own CVSS/EPSS thresholds and contract duration. The live simulator updates `max_payout` and `multiplier` as you adjust.

![Predict page — configure thresholds and see simulated returns before buying](https://assets.polydelve.com/screenshots/predict-simulated-returns.png)

When satisfied, click **Buy**. A confirmation shows your cost in Schmeckles, `max_payout`, and the contract deadline.

### Step 5 — Track your position

Go to **Dashboard** to see your open contracts, Schmeckle balance over time, and per-contract P&L. Check the **Leaderboard** to see where you rank against other players.

![Leaderboard showing Schmeckle balances and win rates](https://assets.polydelve.com/screenshots/qs-5-leaderboard.png)

## What happens next

Contracts resolve automatically — no manual action required.

| Outcome | Condition | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **YES** | A CVE is published for the package in OSV before the deadline | Payout credited to your Schmeckle balance |
| **NO** | Deadline passes with no CVE published in OSV | Contract closes; cost is not refunded |

Schmeckle balance updates on the **Dashboard** immediately after resolution. Check the **Leaderboard** to see where you rank.
